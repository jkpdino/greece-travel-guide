(27‚Äì28 May)

Vibe: Rural farmland, citrus groves, and river valleys; loose, agricultural rhythm.

Planned Core (from Itinerary): Basilica of San Andreas in Patras (May 27), Archaeological Site of Olympia & Olympic Race (May 27), Museum of Olympia (May 28).

Free Time (approximate):

  • May 27 (after hotel check-in and site visit, before dinner): ~18:00 - ~19:30

Food & Drink

  • Enjoy Patras Old Port seafood (if time allows before leaving Patras)
  • Experience farm-to-table dining at Taverna Orestis (Olympia area)
  • Street-food crawl on Agiou Nikolaou steps in Patras (if time allows)
  • Sample pasteli (sesame bars) at a local confectioner in Olympia
  • Riverside picnic under Kladeos plane trees near Olympia

Nature & Outdoors

  • Walk along the Kladeos riverside near Olympia
  • Watch the sunset at Kaiafas Lake (requires a short drive)

  • Sunset kayak or paddle-board on Kaiafas Lake (requires a short drive)

History & Culture

  • Visit the Archimedes Museum in Olympia, showcasing ancient Greek technology.
  • Explore Patras Roman Odeon & lighthouse pier (if time allows in Patras).
  • Climb Patras Castle ramparts at golden hour (if time allows in Patras).
  • Day trip to the Temple of Apollo Epikourios at Bassae (UNESCO site, a longer excursion).

Activities & Experiences

  • Achaia Clauss winery tour (Patras) to taste Mavrodaphne (if time allows).
  • Visit Klio's Honey Farm near Olympia for a tour and tasting.
  • Try a wheel-thrown pottery lesson in Olympia village
  • Visit an olive-wood carving studio in Olympia

Relaxation

  • Hotel pool downtime

Shopping

Corinth & Return Picks

(1 Jun)

Vibe: Industrial corridor with dramatic canal cut; hill-top medieval fort.

Planned Core (from Itinerary): Corinth and Acrocorinth (9:00-11:00 AM), Lunch at Perama (Noon).

Free Time (approximate):

  • Limited time around Corinth itself after scheduled activities.

Flexible Ideas & Options

Food & Drink

  • Enjoy Loukoumades (Greek donuts) and coffee near the Isthmus Canal.
  • Grab spanakopita from Gregory's or other bakery in ATH departures hall.
  • Taste Nemean wines at a local shop in Corinth or Loutraki (if time allows and shops are open).

Nature & Outdoors

  • Quick dip at Kalamaki beach (15 min south of Corinth, if very tight schedule allows before heading to ATH).

History & Culture

  • Tour the Archaeological Museum of Ancient Corinth (confirm if time allows within 9-11 AM slot, or if it's an alternative).
  • Visit Isthmia site & small museum (Temple of Poseidon, near the other end of canal).
  • Inspect Diolkos ancient ship trackway stones near the canal.
  • Snap photos of submerged ruins at Kenchreai port (ancient port of Corinth on Saronic Gulf, requires detour).

Activities & Experiences

  • Corinth Canal pedestrian bridge photo stop (walk the old bridge, watch bungee jumps if active and time allows).
  • Plane-spot from Skybridge observation windows at ATH.

Relaxation

  • Explore the thermal spas of nearby Loutraki for a quick relaxing dip or treatment (time permitting).

Shopping

  • Last-minute olive-wood souvenirs in Corinth market (if open and time allows).
